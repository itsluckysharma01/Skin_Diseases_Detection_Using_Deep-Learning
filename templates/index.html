          How This Classifier Works

          1. Input image is resized to 224x224 and normalized to [0, 1].
          2. EfficientNet-based model extracts visual lesion features.
          3. Softmax layer calculates probability for all 30 classes.
          4. Top-3 confidence classes are shown with next-step guidance.

          Training references and class list are loaded from your notebook output artifacts.
